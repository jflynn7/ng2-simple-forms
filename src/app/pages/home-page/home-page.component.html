What's dis den?

A simple wrapper library for creating forms and form components in Angular 2+.

Rationale.

Forms are complicated, inconsistent and difficult to test.

ng2 Simple Forms aims to provide a simple wrapper for Angular Reactive Forms, to make it easy to create full forms, in an accessible, testable and consistent way.

How?

By exposing a simple interface for creating form elements, and complete forms, ng2-simple-forms allows you to concentrate on more important things, and by implementing auto-generated test IDs (think, Protractor, etc) and accessibility options (configurable, of course), along with WAI compliant mark-up in presentational components, you can generate a form in multiple ways and still end up with a consistent, accessible approach across the project.
